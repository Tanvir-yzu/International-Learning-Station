Grand Opening of YZU International Learning Center

YZU International Learning Center is a student club based on software learning and development. Focus on conducting research work and real-time projects. The opening ceremony for this is going to be held on 21 June, 2024 at Yangzijin Campus, Yangzhou University, China. Every International Student of Yangzhou University is welcome to join the event.
