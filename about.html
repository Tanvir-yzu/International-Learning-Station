About

Overview of the International Learning Station

The International Learning Station Club at Yangzhou University is dedicated to enhancing the proficiency of international students in essential software and programming languages, vital for modern technology and engineering applications. We offer workshops on languages such as C, C++, Java, JavaScript, Python, Go, C#, R, and SQL, along with training in software like AutoCAD, Ansys, SolidWorks, CATIA, Pro/E (Creo), EndNote, MATLAB, Polyflow, ADAMS, and 3D printing. Through hands-on projects, our members gain real-world experience, applying their knowledge and developing problem-solving skills. Faculty can leverage our club for research assistance, either by seeking help or assigning projects to students or the club as a whole. Additionally, we strive to publish cutting-edge research papers, enhancing the university's global recognition and contributing to its academic reputation. By joining our club, students can develop critical skills, gain practical experience, and connect with peers, faculty, and industry professionals.
